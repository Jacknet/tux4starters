General UNIX | What is an Operating System?

When you turn on your computer or phone, the thing that has all of the apps that you use is called an operating system. Operating Systems are the software that support what your computer does, whether it is doing math, playing games, or even making phone calls. Some examples of operating systems that you may have heard of are:

  • Windows - A family of operating systems created by Microsoft and used primarily on personal computers (PCs)
  • macOS - A family of operating systems created by Apple and used on Apple Mac computers
  • Android - A mobile operating system created by Google and the Open Handset Alliance that is used on Android phones
  • iOS - A mobile operating system created by Apple and used on iPhones
